Introduction


An ounce of prevention is worth a pound of cure. Prevention and Prediction is one of the most aspired approaches for curing a disease nowadays. Due to the recent exponential advancement in Information technology, effective disease detection, and prevention methodologies have become a public health priority in many developed countries. Detection of food allergies and allergenic ingredients in food products has received increased attention from the food industry and regulatory agencies. Even a tiny amount of allergy-causing food can trigger signs and severe symptoms or even life-threatening reactions. Thus, a high-quality diet, including functional allergy-free food or functional ingredients, accompanied by physical activity and healthy calorie tracking, will be vital for preventing health problems. This study aims to define the methodologies and approaches of using technological advancements to detect, predict, and prevent food allergies by analyzing the symptoms and ingredients of the consumed food item. In addition, the study intends to keep track of food intake, nutrients, and calories in the food and predict non-communicable diseases with the help of users' daily meal plans. The methodologies and approaches proposed in this research utilize Machine learning, Image processing, and deep Learning inspired by artificial neural networks commonly known as ANN.
